about

I am a PhD Student at UT Austin, advised by David Zuckerman. I received my Bachelor's in Math/Computer Science from UC San Diego, where I was very fortunate to be mentored by Russell Impagliazzo and Mohan Paturi.

My primary interests lie in additive combinatorics, pseudorandomness, and algebraic methods.
